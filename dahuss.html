Business value

Dahuss centralizes the property catalog, presents listings professionally, captures qualified inquiries, and manages inventory from one secure private panel—replacing scattered spreadsheets and disconnected tools.

The problem

A high-end real estate business needs to showcase valuable properties clearly, visually, and with powerful filters. Before this build, listings, images, inquiries, and sales statuses lived in manual or disconnected workflows. Dahuss unifies everything in one web product.

The platform enables:

  • Publish properties for sale or rent
  • Filter by country, zone, type, price, bedrooms, and market
  • Property detail pages with images, features, and inquiry forms
  • Lead capture tied to specific listings
  • Private admin for properties and commercial inquiries

Development methodology

I owned the product end to end—from structure to a deployment-ready platform. Here is how it was built:

  1. 1 — Product architecture

    Public discovery, private admin areas, and modular services for listings, inquiries, and media—organized for scale and a clear split between public and operational workflows.

  2. 2 — Listings & search data

    Structured property and lead information with pricing, location, publish state, galleries, and amenities—designed for fast filtering across countries, zones, and commercial criteria.

  3. 3 — Public experience

    Home, catalog, search, property detail pages, and inquiry forms—with featured listings, text search, commercial filters, and bilingual ES/EN for international audiences.

  4. 4 — Inquiries & business rules

    Inquiry flows with server-enforced validation, consistent responses, and paginated browsing—ensuring leads are tied to real, published listings.

  5. 5 — Admin operations

    Create, edit, publish, feature, or remove properties; review leads and update status—from a protected workspace for the agency team.

  6. 6 — Image workflow

    Optimized uploads with client-side resizing and compression before storage—plus sensible limits on count and file size to keep operations fast and reliable.
